About NSHM Knowledge Campus Kolkata


NSHM Knowledge Campus with a legacy of over 25+ years in providing quality education, with campuses in Kolkata and Durgapur is a leading institute for professional degree courses. NSHM offers multidisciplinary UG & PG programs in Business and Management, Hotel & Tourism Management, Health Sciences, Media, Design, Computing & Analytics, Engineering & Technology, and Nursing. Many new programs like AI & Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, Yoga, Data Science, Fashion Design, & Culinary Science have been added for the students to grab new opportunities.

With a legacy spanning over two decades, our programs provide an academic and professional environment that nurtures holistic development. To broaden the international exposure of the students, NSHM Knowledge Campus has forged a strategic partnership with MUST, Analytics Society Of India, NASSCOM Futureskills, Toonz Animation, The Designers Class (TDC), International Air Transport Association (IATA), Canada, Optometry Council of India, Malaysia University and Science Technology, CINI, IITKGPECELL and many more. Our global collaboration opens doors to a multitude of global opportunities. Students can participate in international global conferences, attend lecture series and live case studies, engage in workshops, and benefit from faculty exchanges. The collaboration also promotes an entrepreneurial mindset and inspires students to become leaders through entrepreneurship.

NSHM has a dedicated cell for Corporate Relations and Talent Transfer (CRTT) to assist the students with demanded training and development for employment, entrepreneurship, and higher study/research in world-class institutions of choice. The dedicated cell plays a crucial role in bridging the gap between students and the corporate world, providing them with the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed in the competitive market. Our esteemed placement partners, include Amazon, Deloitte, Zee Ltd., Coca-Cola, Godrej, Radisson, Nestle, and Emirates among many other

Mission

At NSHM Knowledge Campus, our vision is to create a transformative educational experience for students focused on deep disciplinary knowledge, problem-solving, leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills. We aim to foster a diverse and inclusive community committed to excellence in education, research, and innovation.
